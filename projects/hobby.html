Enjoying the Art of Reading

In order to get the most out of reading you must have a broad range of reading material. First, explore all of the free options such as public library. Reading can get real expensive before you know it. I like the check the library first when a new book comes out. Second, you should have a collection of books in all forms, audio, softback, hardback, and online. It’s good to have reading material for whatever situation you may be in. Whether your outside or inside, on the beach(paperback), in the car(audio), on a plane(notepad), taking a walk(audio) or even on the treadmill(hardback). Third, you should join a book club. It’s good have a community of people that you can experience certain books with. You can refer a good book that you read or you take suggestions from others. Overall sometimes it's better with a like minded community.
